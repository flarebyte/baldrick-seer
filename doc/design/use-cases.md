# Use Cases

Use-case catalog translated from the TypeScript design source.

## Decision and planning

Core decision support and longer-horizon planning problems.

### Decision support

#### General Multi-Criteria Ranking (v1)

Run a scenario-based CLI evaluation that ranks named alternatives from a structured config and emits decision reports for humans and tools.

#### Robust Choice Identification (v2)

Identify alternatives that remain strong across scenarios and under changing assumptions, likely using robustness or sensitivity-oriented post-analysis.

#### Lifecycle Decision Support (v2)

Compare options that must remain effective throughout different stages of organizational or system development.

#### Long-Term Option Evaluation (v2)

Evaluate alternatives that must perform well across multiple possible future environments.

#### Policy Option Analysis (v2)

Support evaluation of policy alternatives where multiple criteria such as impact, feasibility, and cost must be considered.

#### Growth Scenario Evaluation (v1)

Assess the same strategic or technical options under startup, scale-up, and mature-operation scenarios using one shared decision model.

#### Strategic Investment Decision (v2)

Compare investment alternatives considering financial return, risk exposure, and long-term strategic impact.

## Technology and product

Product, platform, and architecture comparisons.

### Technology and product choices

#### System Design Selection (v1)

Compare system designs where trade-offs exist between cost, scalability, and reliability.

#### Product Feature Prioritization (v1)

Rank product features using multiple criteria such as user value, development effort, and strategic importance.

#### Product Roadmap Planning (v2)

Evaluate product initiatives across different market or growth scenarios to support long-term planning.

#### Software Architecture Decision (v1)

Evaluate architectural approaches under different system growth conditions, performance requirements, and reliability expectations.

#### Infrastructure Strategy Planning (v1)

Assess infrastructure alternatives such as cloud providers or deployment models under varying operational scenarios.

#### Technology Platform Selection (v1)

Compare multiple technology platforms across operational scenarios such as startup, scale-up, and enterprise maturity.

## Vendor comparisons

Supplier and service-provider evaluation scenarios.

### Vendors

#### Service Provider Comparison (v1)

Compare service providers where priorities may change depending on scale, regulatory environment, or organizational maturity.

#### Supplier or Vendor Selection (v1)

Evaluate competing suppliers using multiple criteria such as cost, reliability, and service quality under different operating conditions.
